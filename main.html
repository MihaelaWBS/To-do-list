"To Do Lists: Forgetting what you planned to remember since forever!"

To-do lists: the adult version of making promises to yourself that you have no intention of keeping. They're like a roadmap to productivity, except you keep taking detours to the land of procrastination. It's a list of dreams, really - "I dream of cleaning the garage, but I'll start with a nap." And let's not forget the satisfaction of crossing off the easiest tasks first, just to feel accomplished. "Breathe - check! Blink - check!" But hey, they do serve a purpose. They remind us of all the things we'd rather be doing, while we're stuck doing the things we should be doing. Ah, sweet irony!
